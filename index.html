Introduction

Oida aims to make claims transparent, paperless and efficient for both personal and business insurance. Backed by blockchain technology Oida will allow a secure, immutable, distributed and digitised low cost solution. By moving insurance claims onto an immutable ledger, blockchain can help eliminate common sources of fraud in the insurance industry.

Technology

  • Identity Management

    Customer information can be shared among the firms enabling quick customer on-boarding and instant policy issuance to enhance customer experience.

  • Fraud Management

    DLT enables asset provenance and full transaction audit trail to be established within a single source of truth.

  • Smart Contracts

    Using smart contracts we can be sure of the integrity of the business rules and conditions of an insurance policy.
