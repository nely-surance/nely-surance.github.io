Vision

Economical impact: by making insurance claims cost effective for both consumers and insurers.

Environmental impact: by aiming for paperless claims as much as possible.

Oida aims to make claims transparent, paperless and efficient for both personal and business insurance. Backed by blockchain technology Oida will allow a secure, immutable, distributed and digitised low-cost solution. By moving insurance claims onto an immutable ledger, Blockchain can help eliminate common sources of fraud in the insurance industry.
